## About

Nexivo is a desktop application that automatically detects and organizes your games and apps, then launches them with one click. Create custom sessions to open multiple apps in sequence, customize your experience with themes, and let Nexivo learn your usage patterns to surface what you use most.

## Features

- ⚡ **Smart Detection** - Automatically finds your games from Steam, Roblox, Epic Games, and Rockstar Games
- 🎮 **Custom Sessions** - Open multiple apps or websites in sequence with configurable delays
- 🎨 **Themed UI** - Choose from multiple color themes to match your style
- ⌨️ **Hotkey Toggle** - Show or hide the launcher instantly with a customizable global hotkey
- 📊 **Activity Tracking** - Automatically surfaces your most-used items based on launch frequency
- 🔄 **Auto-Updates** - Check for new versions directly from the app

## How to Update

Nexivo checks for updates automatically. In the app:

1. Go to **Settings**
2. Scroll to **Updates** section
3. Click **Check for Updates**

Your app will automatically stay current with new releases.
